# Target teams evolving to a small Service System

The first Autonomous Service release will target small and midsize product teams evolving one modular Lenso application into roughly three to twenty Services in one Operating Region. The product will prioritize safe extraction, contracts, local development, independent delivery, business evidence, and infrastructure adapters over hundreds-of-Service governance, multi-cluster federation, global active-active behavior, or replacement infrastructure.

## Consequences

- A team without a dedicated platform-engineering organization must be able to operate the supported path.
- Clusterless development and Kubernetes-optional deployment remain product requirements.
- The roadmap measures success through a complete Module-to-Service evolution path rather than feature-count parity with enterprise service meshes.
- Large-scale quotas, cost allocation, organization portals, and multi-cluster governance wait for demonstrated user demand.
